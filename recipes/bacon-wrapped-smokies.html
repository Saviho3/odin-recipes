Bacon Wrapped Smokies

Description

Ingridients

  • Bacon
  • Coctail wieners
  • Toothpicks
  • Brown Sugar
  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Prepare the Bacon by slicing into thirds
  3. Wrap the smokies with a piece of bacon
  4. Arrange a baking sheet
  5. Bake the wrapped smokies for 20 to 25 minutes
  6. Serve
